How to Drive Home

I will be driving home for Spring Break to Chesterfield, Virginia. This drive normally takes 3 and 1/2 hours.In order to prepare for this drive, there are a few things that need to be done beforehand.

Driving home can be boring, you should be prepared for this.

  1. Pack all of your bags, and load them into your car.
  2. Fill up your gas tank until it is completely full.
  3. Create a Spotify playlist that will last the 3 Â½ hour drive.
  4. Connect your phone to the bluetooth to play your music.
  5. Enter the address of your destination into your GPS.
  6. Follow the GPS instructions and drive home.
