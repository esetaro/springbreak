How to Study for the LSAT

During Spring I will be studying for the LSAT, so you will be too.

Studying for the LSAT is difficult but the absolute most important factor in applying to Law School. Test books can be expensive so it is important to buy books that are cost effective. I will be taking the LSAT in September but it is important to give yourself plenty of time to study and review.

  1. Purchase the PowerScore LSAT books on Amazon, as well as the LSAC Practice tests and The LSAT Trainer.
  2. Read through the introductory chapters in the trilogy as well as the Trainer.
  3. Take a diagnostic practice test to guage what you need to study and review.
  4. Study the questions types that you missed and aim to receive a top score.
  5. Repeat steps 1-4 until you have mastered the content and feel confident to sit for the exam that will determine your future success in law school.
